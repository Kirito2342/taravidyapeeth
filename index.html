Welcome to Tara Vidyapeeth 

 Tara Vidyapeeth was founded on the DATE with the prime aim of imparting a comprehensive modern education to the children with valuable indian tradition of education, in Kanti & it's surroundings irrespective of caste, creed and community.
                                                                                                                    

Principal's Desk

Tara Vidyapeeth' is an amalgamation of competent teachers, state-of-the-art infrastructure, an idyllic setting and an experienced and efficient administration. The school came into existence with the vision of creating a safe and conducive environment for its students – to provide a perfect balance of academics, sports, artistic and social opportunities. Childhood, youth and learning should be a joy and we strive for that.
